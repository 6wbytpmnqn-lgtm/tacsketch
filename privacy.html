Privacy Policy for TacSketch

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how TacSketch ("the App", "we") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

To function as a collaboration tool for coaches, TacSketch collects the following data:

A. Account Information

When you create an account, we store:

  • Email Address: For authentication and account recovery.
  • Name & Club: To identify you to your team members within the app.
  • User ID: A unique technical identifier linked to your account.

B. User-Generated Content

Materials you create are stored securely to enable synchronization and collaboration:

  • Tactics, drills, and drawings.
  • Training sessions and notes.
  • Team settings (Team Name, Sport, Colors).
  • Player information (Names, Positions, Numbers, optional Photos).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide the App's functionality:

  • Authentication: To securely log you in.
  • Cloud Sync: To save your tactics to the cloud (Firebase Firestore) so you can access them across devices.
  • Collaboration: To allow multiple coaches in the same team to view and edit shared tactics.

3. Third-Party Services & Storage

We do not sell your data. We use the following trusted services to store and sync your data:

  • Google Firebase (Auth & Firestore): Used for secure authentication and real-time database storage for team collaboration. Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple iCloud: To enable seamless access across your devices, some data (such as offline copies of your tactics) is stored in your personal iCloud container. Apple's Privacy Policy.

No Ads or Tracking: TacSketch does not contain third-party advertisements and does not use analytics tools to track you across other apps or websites.

4. Data Deletion & Rights

You have full control over your data.

  • Delete Account: You can delete your account and all associated personal data directly within the App via the Settings menu.
  • Leave/Delete Teams: You can leave teams or, if you are the creator, delete the team and its data entirely.
