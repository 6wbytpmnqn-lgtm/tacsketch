Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TacSketch (“the App”) handles information when you use the App.

1) Summary

  • The App does not require accounts or login.
  • The App stores your content locally on your device and may also store it in your iCloud (iCloud Documents), depending on your device settings.
  • We do not collect, view, or access the content you create in the App.

2) Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.

2.2 App Content

The content you create in the App (for example tactics boards, templates, team/player information, or player photos) is stored:

  • on your device, and/or
  • in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud Documents service (if iCloud is enabled on your device).

This data is associated with your Apple ID and managed by Apple iCloud.

3) iCloud Storage

If iCloud is enabled for the App, your data may be stored in iCloud and synced across your Apple devices.

Important: Deleting the App from your device may not automatically delete data stored in iCloud.

You can manage or delete App data stored in iCloud through your device settings:

Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage (or similar, depending on iOS version).

4) Analytics / Tracking

We do not use tracking technologies or advertising SDKs.

We do not sell or rent any user data.

5) Service Providers

We do not share your App content with third parties.

The App may rely on Apple iCloud services to store/sync your content if enabled.

6) Data Security

We take privacy seriously. Your data is stored locally and/or in iCloud depending on your settings. We do not have access to your iCloud content.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no child data collection.

8) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
